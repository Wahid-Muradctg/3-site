Cultivating Cozier Lives

We believe that a house is not a home until it has a plant breathing life into it. GreenNest was started by two botanists in 2026, aiming to bring sustainable, healthy, and premium nursery stock to busy apartments and city centers.

Every plant in our collection is grown using natural compost, inspected for strong root systems, and acclimatized to standard indoor humidity levels before shipment. This ensures your new plant thrives immediately upon arrival.
