Essential Plant Care Tips

A few simple rules to keep your green companions happy, healthy, and vibrant all year round.

More Care Guides
  • Know Your Soil Moisture Always check the top 2 inches of soil before watering. If dry, water. If damp, wait!
  • Understand Lighting Needs Indirect, filtered bright sun is ideal for 90% of houseplants. Avoid scorching direct sun.
  • Ensure Drainage & Airflow Use pots with drainage holes to avoid root rot. Group plants to elevate ambient humidity.
